Egg Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Piece of Square Bread
  • 1 Slice of Cheese, 2 is Optional

Needed

  • 8 Inch Pan, 6 Inch Pan is Okay

Steps

  1. Put the 8 or 6 Inch Pan in the Stove
  2. Make sure the 2 eggs are Scrambled
  3. Fry the Egg in medium heat pan, dont fully cook it, so the bread can stick to the egg
  4. place the bread on top the the non fully cooked egg, then fully cook the egg
  5. Flip the whole thing
  6. Place a cheese of any piece of the bread, if its 2 place it both on any piece of the bread
  7. Close the sandwich
  8. And keep flipping the sandwich every 10 seconds until you have a desired cooked bread and melted cheese
