Egg Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Piece of Square Bread
  • 1 Slice of Cheese, 2 Slices of Cheese is better, but its optional

Needed

  • 8 Inch Pan, But 6 Inch Pan is Okay

Steps

  1. Put the pan in the stove
  2. Make sure the eggs are scrambled
  3. Fry the egg in medium heated pan, dont fully cook it, so the bread can stick to the egg
  4. Place the bread on top of the non fully cooked egg, after placing the bread, fully cook the egg
  5. Flip the whole thing
  6. Place a cheese of any piece of the bread, if your cheese is 2, place it both on any piece of the bread
  7. Close the sandwich
  8. And keep flipping the sandwich every 10 seconds until you have a desired cooked bread and melted cheese
